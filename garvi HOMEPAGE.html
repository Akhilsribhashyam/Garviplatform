About GARVI

Purpose

GARVI serves as the cornerstone of educational analytics in Gujarat, providing institutions with powerful tools to visualize, analyze, and improve their academic performance through data‑driven insights.

Built under the Gujarat State Institutional Rating Framework (GSIRF), GARVI empowers educational institutions to benchmark their performance, identify improvement areas, and simulate strategic decisions for enhanced academic outcomes.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To establish Gujarat as a leader in educational excellence through data-driven insights, empowering institutions to achieve world-class standards and fostering a culture of continuous improvement in academic performance.

Our Mission
